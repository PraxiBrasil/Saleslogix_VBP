Editing a Product in the List

Only the system administrator and users with an administrator role can access these features.

Use the Product Detail view to edit product information.

To edit

  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Products to open the Products List view. If necessary, use the Lookup to locate a product, and then click on the product name.
  2. If necessary, change the product information.
  3. Click Save.

Customizing Your Personal Workspace Tab

Use the My Workspace tab to view the first five activities scheduled for today and recently viewed items, as well as links to commonly used actions, help topics and Web sites. Depending on your user options, the My Workspace may be the first page you see when you log on to the Sage SalesLogix Web Client.

You can customize your My Workspace tab by adding or removing content, rearranging content, hiding tabs and setting a default tab.

 

How Do I?

Refresh my Personal Workspace content
  • Click for the content you want to refresh.
Open the Personal Workspace menu
  • On a Workspace tab, click .
Rearrange content
  • Use drag and drop to move content on your Workspace tab.
Remove content
  • To remove content from your Workspace tab, click .
Add a tab
  1. Open the Personal Workspace menu.
  • On the My Workspace tab, click
  1. On the menu, click Add Tab.
  2. In the Name box, type a name for the tab.
  3. Click OK.
  4. Add content to the tab.
  5. If necessary, edit your new Personal Workspace tab.
Delete a tab
  1. In your Personal Workspace, click the tab you want to remove.
  2. Open the Personal Workspace menu and click Delete Tab.
  3. Click Yes.
Add content to a tab
  1. Click the Personal Workspace tab where the content will be added.
  2. On the Personal Workspace menu, click Add Content.
  3. Select the check box for each content you want to add.
  4. Click OK.
Edit the appearance of my tab
  1. Click the Personal Workspace tab you want to edit.
  2. On the Personal Workspace tab menu, click Edit Options.
  3. If necessary, in the Name box, type the name you want to display as the tab name.
  4. To make this tab the default tab, select Make this my default page.
  5. Under Choose the template you want to use, select the option that matches the layout you would like to use for this tab. Your choices are:
  • Two columns split
  • Three columns split
  • Two column larger left
  • Two column larger right
  • One column
  1. Click OK.

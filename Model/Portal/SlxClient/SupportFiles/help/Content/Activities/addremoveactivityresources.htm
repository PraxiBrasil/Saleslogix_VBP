Adding and Removing Activity Resources

Use the Resources dialog box to add or remove activity resources when you schedule or edit a phone call or meeting.  

To open the Resources tab
  • Click the Resources tab from the Schedule a Phone Call, Schedule a Meeting, or Edit Activity view.
To add resources
  • From the list of Available Resources, select the resource(s) you want to add, and then click the Add button.
To remove resources
  • From the list of Currently Selected Resources, click the resource(s) you want to remove, and then click the Remove button.

Use CTRL and click to select multiple options.

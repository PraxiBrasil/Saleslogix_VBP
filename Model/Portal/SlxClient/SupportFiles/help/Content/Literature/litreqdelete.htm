Delete a Literature Request

You can delete literature requests from the Activities view, or from the Literature Requests view.

To delete

  1. Do one of the following:
  • Open the Activities List view.
  • Find the contact or account associated with the literature request, open the Contact Detail or Account Detail view, click the Literature Requests tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
  1. Click the Send Date of the item you want to delete.
  2. In the Literature Request Detail view, click the Delete button.
  3. Click OK to permanently delete the record, or Cancel to cancel deletion of the record.

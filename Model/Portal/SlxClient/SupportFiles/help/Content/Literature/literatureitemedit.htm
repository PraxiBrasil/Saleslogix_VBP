Editing a Literature Item

Only the system administrator and users with an administrator role can access these features.

Use the Literature Item Detail view to edit literature item information.

To edit

  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Administration, and then click Literature Items to open the Literature Items List view. If necessary, use the Lookup to locate a literature item, and then click on the item number.
  2. If necessary, change the literature item information.
  3. Click Save.

Adding a Literature Item

Only the system administrator and users with an administrator role can access these features.

You can add documents to the list of available literature. This list is available when scheduling a literature request. You can also edit information about existing documents, such as the quantity on hand.

To add

  1. On the Administration menu, click New Literature Item.
  2. Complete the literature item information boxes.
  1. Click Save.

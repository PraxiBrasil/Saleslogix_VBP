Changing a Password

You have the ability to change your password through the User Options in the Web Client. Once you have made the changes you want, you must log off and then log on again for the changes to take effect.

To change your password

  1. Click Options on the menu bar.
  2. Click the Password tab, and then enter your new password.
  3. Reenter your new password in the Confirm Password field.
  4. Click Save.

    		5.

    You must log out of the Sage SalesLogix Web Client and then log in again before your options changes take effect.

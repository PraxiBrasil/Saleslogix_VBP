Teams Tab

The Teams tab displays the teams of which the department is a member. Use this tab to add or remove a department from a team.

To manage

  1. In the Department Detail view, click the Teams tab.
  2. Do one of the following:
  • Click Add to add the department to a team. Use the Lookup to find and select the appropriate team.
  • Click the Remove link for the team you want to delete the department from.
  1. Click Save.

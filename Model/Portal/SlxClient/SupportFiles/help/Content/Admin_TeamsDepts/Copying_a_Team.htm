Copying a Team

When you copy an existing team, all members are included in the new team.

To copy

  1. Open the Team Detail view for the team you want to copy.
  2. In the Common Tasks pane, click Copy Team.
  3. Click OK to confirm.
  4. In the Name box, type a new name for the team.
  5. If necessary, modify the team members.
  6. Click Save.

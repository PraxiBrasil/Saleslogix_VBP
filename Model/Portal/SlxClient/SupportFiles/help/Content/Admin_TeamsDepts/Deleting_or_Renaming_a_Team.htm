Deleting or Renaming a Team

Use the Team Detail view to delete or rename a team.

How Do I?

Delete a Team

  1. Open the Team Detail view of the team you want to delete.
  2. Click Delete.

Rename a Team

  1. Open the Team Detail view of the team you want to rename.
  2. In the Name box, type a new name for the team.
  3. Click Save.

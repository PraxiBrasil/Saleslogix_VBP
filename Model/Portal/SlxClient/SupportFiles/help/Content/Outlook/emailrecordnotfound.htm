Contact(s)/Lead(s) Not Found

The Contact(s)/Lead(s) Not Found view appears when you are recording an e-mail message from Outlook to Sage SalesLogix history, and the contact or lead e-mail address is not in the Sage SalesLogix database. The Contact(s)/Lead(s) Not Found view shows the subject and to whom the e-mail is being sent.

The e-mail will not be recorded to history in Sage SalesLogix.

How Do I?

Set e-mail log to history options

Prevent this dialog box from displaying in the future

  • Do not prompt when Contact(s)/Lead(s) not found – select this option if you do not want to see the Contact(s)/Lead(s) Not Found view.

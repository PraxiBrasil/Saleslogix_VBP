Technical Support

Technical Support is available to customers who purchased their support contracts directly from Sage and to Certified Business Partners. Calls are answered during business hours, Monday through Friday, excluding holidays. Current contact information is available on the Sage SalesLogix Web site.

Customers with a valid technical support contract and a Web access code can request technical support electronically on the Sage SalesLogix SupportOnline/Sage Software Online Support and Services Web site.
